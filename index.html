Our Mission

One Weekend
a Month.
Two Missions.

The New Jersey Army National Guard serves a dual mission: providing trained, ready units to support the U.S. Army overseas — and protecting life and property here at home during natural disasters and civil emergencies.

As citizen-soldiers, our Guardsmen maintain civilian careers and lives while remaining ready to answer the call whenever and wherever needed — neighbors, colleagues, and protectors of the Garden State.

⚔️

Federal Mission

Supporting active Army operations and national defense worldwide.

Why Serve

More Than a Weekend

Serving in the NJNG comes with world-class benefits that support you, your education, and your family — building a foundation that lasts your entire career and beyond.

🎓

100% Tuition Waiver

Full tuition at any NJ state college through the NJ National Guard Tuition Program.

💰

Enlistment Bonuses

Up to $20,000 for qualifying MOS positions and prior-service members joining the Guard.

🏥

Healthcare

Access to TRICARE health coverage for you and your family during activated service.

🏦

Military Retirement

Pension starting at age 60 after 20 qualifying years of service in the National Guard.

🎽

Skills Training

Technical and leadership credentials that advance your civilian career and professional growth.

🏠

VA Home Loans

VA-backed home loans with no down payment required after qualifying service periods.

✈️

Travel & Adventure

Train and deploy across the U.S. and worldwide alongside fellow NJ citizen-soldiers.

🛡️

Split Training Option

Attend Basic Training between your junior and senior year of high school — finish school before AIT.
